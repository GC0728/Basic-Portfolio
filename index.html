About Me





My coding journey started from humble beginnings. I used to be the fly on the wall as my friends would codespeak for hours. I would hear them say words such as "array" and "object" and be completely lost. This would go on for what felt like hours at a time. I would look around to the other people conversing with us who also neither understood what was being said and together all of us would just stare with a blank look on our face as we were simultaneously shaking our heads. You could say that my journey to learn how to write a programming language began with a desire to have a conversation with the people that I considered my friends.

Prior to immersing myself in the ways of Code Fu I worked as a Paralegal. Working as a Paralegal, I've acquired skills that will mold me into a web developer that is client-focused and quality-focused.
